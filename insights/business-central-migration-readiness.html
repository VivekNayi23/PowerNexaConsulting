Preparing Legacy Invoice Data for Business Central Migration

A structured approach to mapping, cleansing, validating, importing, and reconciling legacy customer and vendor transactions.

Strong delivery begins with clear business thinking. Use the following guidance to improve decisions before implementation.

Separate master data from transactions

Customers, vendors, items, accounts, dimensions, terms, and posting groups must be ready first.

Decide what should remain historical

Choose whether to migrate open transactions, balances, or detailed history.

Map documents and lines together

Ensure document numbers, references, dates, amounts, dimensions, and line details remain consistent.

Validate posting dependencies

Posting groups, number series, currencies, dimensions, locations, and accounts can block migration.

Reconcile before go-live

Compare record counts, totals, open balances, and control accounts.

Use technology to strengthen a well-understood process—not to hide an unclear one.
